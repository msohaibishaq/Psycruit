About Us

An intelligent Teacher Evaluating Chatbot



This software system will be a Psychology aware Chat-bot based Recruitment System for assisting different schools in their recruitment process.

The system will highlight the importance of psychological evaluation before hiring for a teaching job. The candidates for the job will answer some questions while chatting with our chat-bot on our website. This process will better the recruitment system and make it more efficient.

Education is one of the most important sectors of our society. However, it is not given the importance it needs and some major shortcomings go unnoticed due to which our educational institutes do not yield the best productivity. One such factor is the psychological compatibility of the teachers which is ignored while hiring. The institutes at the primary and secondary levels cannot hire a psychologist only for recruitment. No such system exists which can perform this job. The education system can be made more efficient if such evaluation is done while the hiring is done. Our motive is to build a chat-bot that can psychologically evaluate the candidates and give us the best one for the job.
